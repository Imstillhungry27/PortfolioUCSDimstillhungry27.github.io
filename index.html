Backdrops

Here are a couple examples of commissioned backdrops I created, which generally are made on a larger scale with more detail. They are products of well-communicated collaborative effort between me and the client.

Echo Park

The client had asked to create a scene inspired by this local park in LA, and specifically requested a black and white comic-inspired style. This design was created on Procreate and was printed into a 8 by 10 feet cloth picture backdrop used for photos.

Reverent Gratitude

This was created to be a visually aesthetic background for song lyrics. It followed the theme of ethereal space and light reflections. There were many different iterations in different sizes that were also used for social media promotion and an album cover. This backdrop remained digital, and was created with Adobe Photoshop and Procreate.

About Me

Ava Kam is a versatile and creative studio artist who specializes in illustration, digital art and graphic design. She was born and raised in Southern California and is currently attending the University of California San Diego (2026).

Ava has predominantly been working in graphic design and has over 4 years of experience.She finds inspiration in pop culture and various sports she enjoys, including swimming and archery. She also has a background in UX/UI design and has experience with front-end development and visual design.
